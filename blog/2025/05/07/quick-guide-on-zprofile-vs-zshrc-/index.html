    Use both in tandem: keep your environment reliable with ~/.zprofile, and your shell pleasant and tweakable with ~/.zshrc ‚ú®

    3. Practical Rules of Thumb üìù

    1. Session vs. Prompt ‚è±Ô∏è If it should happen once per session, choose ~/.zprofile; if it should affect every new prompt, choose ~/.zshrc.

    2. PATH-mangling early? üõ£Ô∏è Put it in ~/.zprofile so every child process inherits it.
